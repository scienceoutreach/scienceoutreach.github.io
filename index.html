Who we are

Science Outreach is an independent student run science news portal. The basic aim of this project is to develop the culture of science journalism and to convey ongoing &useful research to common man in a lucid language through reporting.Another purpose of this project is to develop popular scientific writting among different streams of Science and Mathematics. The tagline of Science Outreach clearly specifies its roles ,popularising the news, articles, science features and fiction related to science. For this we need volunteer for various tasks.If you are interested in reporting, writing or management we have platform for you.
